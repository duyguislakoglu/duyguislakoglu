About me

I am a PhD student in Machine Learning in the Declarative Languages and Artificial Intelligence group at KU Leuven, where I conduct research under the supervision of Professor Hendrik Blockeel since October 2014.

My main research interest include statistical relational learning, probabilistic graphical models, missing data and deep learning. For my PhD topic, I focus on combining statistical relational learning and deep learning on the problem of statistical predicate invention.
