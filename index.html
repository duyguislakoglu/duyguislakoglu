Sebastijan Dumančić

Assistant Professor in Artificial Intelligence
CV

I am an Assistant Professor in the Algorithmics group at the Delft University of Technology.

I design machines that learn and reason. My goal is to develop machines that can learn as efficiently as humans, as well as apply their knowledge as flexibly. In pursuing this goal, I contribute to and use techniques from program induction/synthesis, inductive logic programming, probabilistic programming and statistical relational AI. My work is largely inspired by ideas and progress in cognitive science.

Before joining Delft University of Technology, I was an FWO-funded postdoctoral fellow in the DTAI lab at KU Leuven working with Hendrik Blockeel and Luc De Raedt. I obtained my PhD from KU Leuven in 2018 under the supervision of Hendrik Blockeel. Before that, I was a student at the University of Zagreb.
